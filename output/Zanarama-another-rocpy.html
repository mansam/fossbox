Another RocPy

Sat 26 October 2013
By Zanarama

In legacy.

tags: legacyfoss@rit

The RocPy meetup for October mostly consisted of lightning talks from students in the HFOSS course with our project ideas. It was a little boring in terms of presentation, mostly because they are only ideas at this point; nothing has been implemented.

My group gave our talk on the WeatherStation activity we hope to make. Our presentation can be found here. There seemed to be a real interest in the sensors there, which is no surprise to me because hardware is AWESOME! Most people interested in computers deal almost exclusively with software. I think it is really neat how receptive everyone has been to our hardware based project that is a bit different than the normal projects.
