A guest from Sugar came to visit today. He had a lot of interesting things to say and was very interested in our work with the open badges for the Sugar Environment. What I worked on was finishing a way to allow players to create their own custom recipes. That is finished. However, after showing it to the rest of the group they had some suggestions of things I should fix. It shouldn't take me more than an hour or two. Then after that I'm going to start working on some badges things.
