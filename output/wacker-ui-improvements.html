Thu 20 June 2013
By wacker

In legacy.

tags: legacyfoss@rit

These were my first two tickets I have closed on GitHub!

As for now, I will be working on implementing our menu screens and difficulty selection functionality. I will upload pictures later on of the new UI and how it looks on the XO laptop!
