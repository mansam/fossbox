Lets do some translations

Thu 11 July 2013
By wacker

In legacy.

tags: legacyfoss@rit

For the past week I have been hacking on Lemonade Stand changing all of our hard coded text into dynamic text for loading in different dynamic sets of stands. While doing this, I have changed all the text to gettext to be able to translate the game into Spanish as well as other languages. Currently, we have a Spanish version of Lemonade Stand working.

Similar to Lemonade Stand, I finished working on Sky Time and changed all of our text to gettext and is ready to be translated for a Spanish version. This should be up and running within the next day or two.
