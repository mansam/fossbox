tags: legacyfoss@rit

I today trying to close one of the tickets for Lemonade Stand. The ticket asked for a version of lemonade stand with strawberries and ice. Strawberries were already somewhat in the game but commented out, but I had to create ice from scratch, getting all new images from FOSS@RIT's resident artist. After that I customized the recipes and added some new events to help fully integrate the new ingredient into the game. However while testing it out I noticed that even though I changed the recipe the game was still selling only the basic lemonade.
