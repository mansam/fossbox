Day 3

Sun 02 June 2013
By thengrad

In legacy.

tags: legacyfoss@rit

We spent the day mostly moving everything we had packed up on day 2 to the new location for the FOSSBox for the summer. It was a lot of tiring working moving all the heavy and awkward equipment. There were a few injuries during moving but luckily there was nothing serious. There were a few stiff backs, a couple people got minor cuts, and I ended up with splinters. However, the moving went very smoothly. The rest of the day, us in the Lemonade Stand team went over our ideas of what we want to work on with and split them into what we will finish, what we will try to finish if we have time, and what we would like to see finished in the future with one of the mentors for the project, Prof. Jacobs.
